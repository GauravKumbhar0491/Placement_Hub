Find answers to the most common queries about Placement Hub.

The College Placement Hub website is an online platform dedicated to connecting students and alumni with job and internship opportunities. It serves as a centralized hub for accessing career resources, job listings, and recruitment events.
Students can explore internship and job opportunities, upload resumes for employers to view, schedule career advising appointments, and access resources such as resume templates, interview tips, and networking events.
Yes, students need to register with their college email IDs to access the full range of features. Registration is free and allows students to create profiles, apply for jobs, and receive notifications.
The platform caters to a diverse range of industries and employers, both local and national. Job listings vary based on employer partnerships and industry demand, covering IT, finance, manufacturing, and more.
Yes, employers can view student profiles and reach out to them directly regarding job opportunities, internships, or networking events. Students must keep their profiles updated to maximize visibility.
The Placement Hub team verifies employer accounts and reviews job postings to ensure they meet quality standards and are relevant to students' career interests. Students can also provide feedback on their experiences.

Yes, alumni are encouraged to utilize the resources and job listings available. They can register using their alumni email IDs or contact the alumni relations office for assistance.
Job listings are regularly updated as new opportunities become available. Students are encouraged to check the website frequently and set up job alerts to stay informed about the latest openings.
Students can reach out to the college's career services office or the website's support team for technical assistance, troubleshooting, or general inquiries. Contact information is available on the college's website.
